Why schools partner with Safe Loop

When a school partners with Safe Loop, they’re not just adopting a product — they’re making a statement that child safety is non-negotiable. Here’s what that partnership looks like.

  1. 01

    Real-Time Bus Tracking

    Parents see exactly where the bus is, who is driving, and when their child will arrive — live, on a map, every single day.

  2. 02

    Smart Onboarding

    When a school partners with us, every parent gets a seamless onboarding experience. They select their ward’s school, choose their route, and they’re set — in under two minutes.

  3. 03

    Complete Route Visibility

    Every stop, every turn, every route path — fully visible to parents. No guessing. No anxiety.

  4. 04

    Multi-Child, Multi-School Support

    Families with children across different schools can manage everything from a single app. One parent. One dashboard. Complete peace of mind.

  5. 05

    Instant Alerts & Timeline

    Pickup, drop-off, delays, route changes — parents get notified at every step. A full daily timeline replaces the uncertainty.

  6. 06

    A Growing Ecosystem

    Bus tracking is just the beginning. We’re building towards attendance integration, campus entry/exit monitoring, emergency SOS, and more. Every school that partners with Safe Loop today becomes a founding part of this ecosystem.
